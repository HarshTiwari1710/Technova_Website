WHY WORK WITH DATAPOOL CLUB?

What We Do?

The Datapool Club intends to help students convert data into insights, to help solve real world problems. In addition, the students are introduced to various topics of their interest such as Database Management System, languages like MySQL, data collection techniques etc. Moreover, the club invites enthusiastic students to study and observe available data and achieve key information to solve world problems in their own unique way. We organize various Seminars & Webinars, where renowned Data Analysts enlighten the students about various future opportunities in the data science field. We maintain a healthy competition between students and the best ones are decided by factors such as participation in presentations, deadline commitments & project completions.

Love Babbar Event 2022

Datapool club in collaboration with GitHub club worked together to plan an fabulous event on October 7, 2022 featuring the pious coder, dedicated teacher and passionate vlogger, Love Babbar. The event was a fan favorite among the students of Sharda University and a high engagement was shown from the audience. He went on to discuss his experience as a student and answered several students queries. We thank Love Babbar for his time and making this event a massive hit. A warm appreciation shown to all involved in making this event as great as possible.
