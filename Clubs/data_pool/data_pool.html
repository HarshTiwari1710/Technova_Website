WHY WORK WITH DATA POOL CLUB

What We Do?

The Datapool Club intends to help students convert Data into Insights, to help solve real world problems. In addition, the Students are introduced to various topics of their Interest such as Database Management System, Languages like MySQL, Data Collection Techniques etc. Moreover, the Club invites enthusiastic students to study and observe available data and achieve key information to solve world problems in their own unique way.We organize various Seminars & Webinars, where renowned Data Analysts enlighten the students about various future opportunities in the Field.We maintain a healthy competition between students and best ones are decided by factors such as Participation in presentations, Deadline commitments & Project completions.
