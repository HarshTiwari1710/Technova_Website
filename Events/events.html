Love Babbar Event 2022

DataPool and Github Club worked together to plan an fabulous event on October 7, 2022 featuring the pious coder, dedicated teacher and passionate vlogger, Love Babbar. The event was a fan favorite among the students of Sharda University and a high engagement was shown from the audience. He went on to discuss his experience as a student and answered several students queries. We thank Love Babbar for his time and making this event a massive hit. A warm appreciation shown to all involved in making this event as great as possible.

GDSC hosted an event on Google CrowdSource for the entire first and second year students. We were joined by a prolific speaker Nikhil Madhusudan Raichur Raichur, the program manager at Google Crowdsource, to give us wonderful insights regarding the crowdsource program.
